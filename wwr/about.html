History

Born from a passion for adrenaline and adventure, Xtreme Rafting was founded by a group of thrill-seekers who wanted to share their love for wild rivers with others. From the very beginning, we have been committed to delivering unforgettable experiences, combining safety, excitement, and breathtaking landscapes. With expert guides and top-quality equipment, we take our guests on thrilling rides through the most intense rapids, always ensuring a safe and exhilarating adventure.

Whether you're a beginner looking for your first rush of adrenaline or a seasoned rafter ready for your next challenge, Xtreme Rafting has the perfect experience for you. Are you ready to paddle into adventure?
